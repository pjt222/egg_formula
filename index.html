Universal Egg Shape Formula

Visualization of the formula from "Egg and math: introducing a universal formula for egg shape" by Narushin, Romanov & Griffin (2020).

The egg profile is defined by four measurable parameters: L (length), w (max width), B (asymmetry distance), and DL4 (diameter at quarter length). The 3D shape is generated as a surface of revolution around the longitudinal axis.
